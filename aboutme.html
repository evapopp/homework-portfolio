About Eva Popp

Why Coding?

I hail from the midwest (yep I'm a Sconnie) where I grew up playing every sport my parents would enroll me in. I continued on to play soccer in college and decided to pursue my love for physical activity and sport by majoring in Kinesiology. Upon graduation, I moved to Oregon and worked in the physical therapy field for five years. During that time I found myself interested in the tech field, yet feeling quite intimidated and underqualified. After hearing about coding bootcamps and their embracement of students with little to no experience, I decided to enroll with University of Oregon's Full Stack program and pursue a career in web development.


Who Am I Outside of Class?

I have completely fallen in love with Oregon and all that it holds, and I am always itching to explore more. Therefore, you can usually find me camping in the mountains, hiking new trails, or adventuring on bike.

My other love is ultimate frisbee. In addition to competing on an elite women's team based in Portland, I was also signed to a women's professional team and made the US Women's National Team in 2020.
